What should I enter in the Debt Snowball Calculator?

Use figures from the same time period and keep every monetary amount in the same currency.

Does this tool include taxes?

No. Results are planning estimates before taxes unless an input explicitly accounts for them.

Can I use a currency other than dollars?

Yes. The arithmetic is currency-neutral; treat the dollar symbol as your chosen currency and stay consistent.

Why might my real result differ?

Actual lenders may calculate interest daily and apply payments differently. Rounding and changing future inputs can also affect the outcome.

How often should I update the estimate?

Recalculate whenever a major input changes so the comparison remains useful.
