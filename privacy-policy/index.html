Data retention and security

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. We use reasonable safeguards, but no internet transmission or storage method is completely secure.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or withdrawal of consent. You may contact us to make a request. We may need to verify your identity before completing it.
