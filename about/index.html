About

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Toolbox is a growing collection of practical online tools designed to make everyday calculations and routine tasks quicker. Instead of searching through complicated software, you can find a focused tool and get a clear result directly in your browser.

What we build

Our collection includes calculators and utilities for finance, business, health, education, real estate, marketing, logistics, conversions, and other common needs. Each tool is designed around a specific task so that inputs and results remain easy to understand.

Our approach

We aim to keep Toolbox fast, accessible, and straightforward on both desktop and mobile devices. We review tools for usability and calculation accuracy, but users should independently verify any result that affects an important financial, medical, legal, academic, or business decision.

Suggestions and corrections

If you notice an error or have an idea for a useful tool, please contact us. Clear feedback helps us improve existing tools and decide what to build next.
